Connect

Lowis & Gellen expanded their chicago presence to the 20th floor at 200 west adams. With increased client communication at the forefront of their project goals, the design encourages interaction and communication through its variety of spatial arrangements.

Engage

Lowis & Gellen features an open plan that utilizes art glass and strong lighting elements to create a simple and approachable space. As you enter, warm wood tones take you from reception into the conferencing areas. The ‘Lowis & Hellen’ red accent carries through the space in small accents, enhancing their brand identity. The combination of bold red accents, textured wallpaper, and soft cove lighting, create a sophisticated envelope for the work of such a prestigious law firm.
